Old school values;

We have no repair deeds. This was done to give the crafters a stronger role in the society. If you need armor, or just need armor repaired, get to know a good crafter. Not everyone wants to invest the time into building a A good crafter.

For those that do, they will build a loyal following.

Total skills cap set to 800.

The thoughts here is, if we keep the standared 5 skills at 120 max with a powerscroll. We can have one extra skill at 80. This was to help the templets that are slap full. Keeping a roleplay feel to the template. A temlplate hase no identity when you take away skillcaps all together.

With no skill caps every character can do everything, compleetly looseing there identity.
